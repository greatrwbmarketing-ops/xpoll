## What we're building

XPoll is a decentralized human signal oracle that lets AI agents and distributed teams coordinate, fund, verify, and reward work — without companies, cap tables, or traditional institutions.

It extends existing data infrastructure (e.g. Snowflake) into agent-consumable economic systems, bridging off-chain intelligence with on-chain coordination.

## How XPoll addresses these

XPoll's patent-pending infrastructure replaces traditional coordination systems with a tokenized data economy built on human-verified signal.

Participants create *campaigns* (tasks, ideas, or initiatives) and attach tokenized incentives. Users and AI agents contribute by providing responses, data, or actions, validated through structured polling, consensus, and behavioral data trails.

Each participant builds a persistent, verifiable identity through **soulbound data profiles**, allowing both humans and AI agents to establish credibility without institutional backing.

Funding flows through **Data Access Tokens (DATs)**, which serve as both access rights and economic instruments. Value generated from data usage is automatically redistributed through a treasury system, enabling continuous compensation without exits, acquisitions, or corporate structures.

AI agents operate within this system using controlled payment rails — they can autonomously purchase data, contribute work, and participate in coordination while maintaining cost governance and transparency.

The result is a self-sustaining coordination layer where:

- capital is deployed without equity
- ownership is defined without cap tables
- trust emerges from data
- value persists beyond the lifecycle of any individual project

## Biggest blocker right now

XPoll has a working system for capturing and monetizing human signal. The layer we're advancing now is **deterministic, on-chain verification and dispute resolution** — reputation-weighted consensus and programmable arbitration so both human and AI participants can operate in a fully trustless environment.

This is also why we don't fully address Problem #6 yet: it's the next thing we're building, and where conviction + capital + collaboration would move us fastest.
